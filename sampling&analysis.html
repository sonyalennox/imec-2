Sampling & Analysis

iMEC has extensive experience in selecting, commissioning and configuring a variety of gas and liquid hydrocarbon analyzer and sampling systems. The key to designing these systems is selecting the correct analyzer type, sample collection, and handling systems. Our expertise lies in liquids terminals, refineries and natural gas receipt and delivery stations.

Planning phase – Choosing the correct analyzer is critical to the project success. iMEC can assist with the analyzer selection based on product and process conditions, vendor support and maintenance requirements of which effect analyzer reliability and OPEX costs.

Execution phase - iMEC will work with client process engineers and EPCs during detailed design to ensure analyzer installations are orchestrated correctly prior to IFC (Issued For Construction) drawings. We will provide analyzer/sampling system subject matter experts to ensure the equipment selected during the planning phase is being properly integrated into the design build.

Turnover to Operations

As a strategic partner, iMEC will provide operational training and/or maintenance support before and after the analyzer package is commissioned. Using Intergraph Smart® Completions, we will create a foundation of information with traceability and accountability for control and work processes providing seamless transitions to operations and maintenance. A baseline performance curve will be created for new or recently maintained analyzer sampling system installations. Implementation of SOPs, and effective service programs have proven to dramatically reduce down time and annual maintenance costs.

iMEC will provide a complete analytical and sampling system assessment of your facilities to ensure existing systems are being maintained and are functioning reliably.
