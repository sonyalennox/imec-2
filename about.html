About iMEC

Accurate, reliable measurement and allocation processes are fundamental to all aspects of a successful oil and gas business. iMEC has deep experience in Gain/Loss and Lost-and-Unaccounted-For (LUF) product investigations, from creating schematics of balance segments to transport induced errors (via SCADA or other data collection systems) to installation effects and procedural missteps. In conjunction with your company’s field operations staff, we are strategically structured to manage your outsourcing requirements on a part time or full time basis. iMEC will ensure your organization is compliant with local government regulations.

iMEC's commissioning team was awarded three turbochargers in the country of Mexico that together exceed 135,000 horsepower as well as the Altamira V measuring station of the Southern De Texas – Tuxpan gas pipeline. More recently, the planning, coordination and support of a liquids terminal facility in San Louie Potosi consisting of unit train loading of gas and diesel products with a common header to 35 loading spots. Our one team approach, consisting of mechanical, electrical, instrumentation, automation and measurement expertise utilized Hexagon’s Smart Completions software (formerly IBS Continuum Edge) to manage the commissioning and improve efficiencies by as much as 20%.

With growing emphasis on the environment, we have partnered with some of the best in the industry and can assist your organization in the form of emissions testing, leak detection, analysis and reporting to meet expanding regulatory requirements, including air pollutant and methane emissions regulations. The leadership team at iMEC combined with our subject matter experts bring over 160 years of combined experience in the Oil & Gas Industry.
