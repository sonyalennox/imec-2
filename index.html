Power Generation

As the power generation industry becomes more efficient so does your plant. You need consistent and reliable energy in an already heavily regulated market. Commissioning a power generation facility after intense inspection is critical to its life cycle from which performance, availability and maintenance is dependent. Good facility design, proper choice of equipment and project execution are all basic aspects for any new industrial installation to achieve the production values expected. Between the design, construction and full operation of the plant, there is a process which will mark the installation’s lifespan. iMEC’s experienced commissioning team can help.

Oil & Gas

Accurate, reliable measurement and allocation processes are fundamental to all aspects of a successful oil and gas business, from reservoir modelling and management to interfacing with third parties and meeting fiscal and reporting obligations. iMEC has deep experience in Gain/Loss and Lost-and-Unaccounted-For (LUF) investigations, from balanced segments to transport induced errors (via SCADA or other data collection systems) to installation effects and procedural missteps. In conjunction with your company’s field operations staff, iMEC can ensure that your organization is compliant with local government regulations.

Transmission

iMEC's consultants are some of the most highly recommended in North America, with the expertise to handle the most challenging applications related to analyzers, meters, RTUs, and associated peripheral devices. Audit assessments to regulatory and industry standards, quality control plans and procedures are just the beginning. iMEC's commissioning team was awarded three turbochargers for TC Energy in the country of Mexico that together exceed 135,000 horsepower as well as the Altamira V measuring station of the Southern De Texas – Tuxpan gas pipeline. Our team consisting of mechanical, electrical, instrumentation, automation and measurement expertise utilized Hexagon’s Smart Completions software (formerly IBS Continuum Edge) to manage the commissioning and improve efficiencies by as much as 20%.
