Measurement & Allocation

Accurate, reliable measurement and allocation processes are fundamental to all aspects of a successful oil and gas business, from reservoir modelling and management to interfacing with third parties and meeting fiscal and reporting obligations. iMEC has deep experience in Gain/Loss and Lost-and-Unaccounted-For (LUF) investigations, from creating schematics of balance segments to transport induced errors (via SCADA other data collection systems) to installation effects and procedural missteps. In conjunction with your company's field operations staff, iMEC can ensure that your organization in compliant with local government regulations.

Additional Services

  • Allocation and audit of transported volumes
  • Gap analysis and compliance with API, NEP, ISO and AGA
  • Manage custody transfer processes and documentation
  • Intelligent planning and expert commissioning services
  • Investigate, identify and mitigate transportation losses; defend against litigation if required
  • Provincial government EPAP reporting and compliance managment
  • Measurement schematics development
  • Pipeline and plant imbalance assessments
